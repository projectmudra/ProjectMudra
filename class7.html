C L A S S - 7

A video can be a powerful educational and motivational tool, and YouTube is now increasingly being used by educators as a pedagogic resource. To facilitate the Government School students to access the lectures in various languages is the main objective behind our YouTube channel. The team has uploaded Science, English, Social Studies, and Agriculture videos in Kannada, English, and Telugu languages to benefit peer students. We also encourage the students to post their doubts in the comment section to clear them. Thus, we have utilized this platform to promote active viewing and maximize learning.
