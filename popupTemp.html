At Project Mudra, we teach at various schools to spread the joy of learning and education. The below our some of the schools we teach in:
MPUC School located in Ananth Nagar, was the first school taken up by Project Mudra. Vedic mathematics, Spoken English and Computer Applications are taught here, and the children are divided into two sections- one with the students who know kannada and the other who know English.
Sarlabettu school is located near the Uma Maheshwari Temple in Manipal. In addition to basic mathematics, computer applications and spoken english, we also teach life skills, art and craft and mental ability. Although this school lacks basic facilities, the students of Sarlabettu are extremely enthusiastic and full of passion making it one of the best schools to teach in.
Indrali High School located in Udupi is home to highly Interactive Students with good communication skills. Members and Volunteers of Project Mudra had a pleasent Experience conductiong a science fair here back in 2019.

Project Mudra is also a part of many Initiatives. Such as:

Khwayishon Ki Roshni

Khwayishon Ki Roshni was an initiative taken by Project Mudra wherein donors could donate their books/stationery in leiu of diyas painted meticulously by the students of Madhava Kripa School. The collected books and stationery were distributed to the children of bijapur slum.

Think Pink

Think Pink was an initiative by Project Mudra to bring about awarennes regarding self testing, early detection and ongoing research for Breast Cancer among SchoolChildren. The campaign held via Goolge Meet featured an interactive classroom experience offered to schools across Andhra Pradesh and Karnataka.
